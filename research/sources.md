# Research Sources: YouTube Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

Collected on: 2026-06-19  
Repository purpose: collect high-signal practitioner material that can support a later playbook on using YouTube as a B2B SaaS pipeline, trust, and distribution channel.

## Selection Criteria

I chose practitioners who either run B2B/SaaS YouTube programs, advise SaaS teams on video strategy, operate B2B content engines, or publish repeatable thinking about turning long-form video into revenue-supporting assets. I intentionally avoided generic "content marketing" voices unless they had a concrete video, YouTube, podcast, or distribution system behind their advice.

## Collection Inventory

- `research/linkedin-posts/`: public LinkedIn post records organized by expert. Each file includes source URLs, observed dates, concise summaries, and relevance notes.
- `research/youtube-transcripts/`: transcript-derived notes organized by video. I used `youtube-transcript-api` where available and stored summaries, metadata, and short excerpts rather than full copied transcripts.
- `research/other/`: collection method notes and additional non-LinkedIn/non-YouTube materials such as reports, podcast pages, and articles.

## Notes on Dates

YouTube upload dates are exact where `yt-dlp` metadata was available. LinkedIn dates are recorded as observed public page labels, for example "LinkedIn reported: 2 months ago; observed on 2026-06-19", because LinkedIn often exposes relative dates on public pages.
