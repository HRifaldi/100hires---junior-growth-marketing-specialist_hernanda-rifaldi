## Transcript-Derived Notes

- Samu audits FullFunnel.io as an example of a B2B company with strong LinkedIn presence but underdeveloped YouTube execution.
- The main critique is that the channel is being used as a home for live-show and podcast reuploads rather than as a YouTube-first lead generation channel.
- He frames the strategy as generalizable to B2B companies: start from the ICP, identify problems they already search for, and package the content for YouTube discovery.
- The video emphasizes titles, thumbnails, hooks, story flow, and CTAs as part of the product, not as last-minute upload tasks.
- LinkedIn content and founder expertise can be used as raw material, but the final YouTube video still needs platform-native structure.

## Playbook Takeaways

- Do not import a LinkedIn/podcast format directly into YouTube.
- Build videos around buyer problems and search/browse behavior.
- Treat packaging and retention as strategic work, not creative decoration.
